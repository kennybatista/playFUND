Expanded Game Library

playFUND is tirelessly working to expand our game offerings. We expect to experience rapid growth and have 20 additional games developed by Winter 2015. We also appreciate user reccommendations! If you would like to make a game suggestion, please write to us via our Contact page. Thanks!

Donation Matches

While a few organizations pledge to match all donations made to their respective causes, we aim to have this become a standard agreement with all of our partners by Winter 2015. Currently, donations are 100% matched by Doctor's Without Borders, the Amazing Day Foundation, Autism Speaks, and Human Rights Watch.
